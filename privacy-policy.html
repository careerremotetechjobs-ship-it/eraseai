Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective immediately

🔒
The short version: EraseAI processes all images directly in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server, never stored, and never shared with anyone — not even us. We have zero access to your images. All tools are $0 forever.

1Who We Are

EraseAI ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at eraseai.pages.dev, providing a free suite of AI-powered browser-based image tools — background remover, image resizer, compressor, converter, cropper, and background color changer. All tools are free at $0.

2Image Data — The Most Important Part

EraseAI is built with a privacy-first architecture. When you upload an image:

  • Your image is processed entirely within your own web browser using WebAssembly and local AI models.
  • Your image is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party server.
  • Your image is never stored, cached, logged, or retained in any form.
  • The AI model (RMBG-1.4) runs locally on your device — no cloud processing occurs.
  • When you close the browser tab, all image data is immediately discarded.

We technically cannot view your images because they never leave your device. This is by design.

3Information We Collect Automatically

Like all websites, we collect basic non-personal technical data through hosting infrastructure and analytics:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visit, referring URL.
  • Usage analytics: General usage patterns via Google Analytics (anonymised). We use this to improve the tools.
  • Device information: Browser version, operating system, screen resolution for compatibility purposes.

4Cookies

EraseAI uses minimal cookies. We do not use cross-site tracking cookies.

  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics places cookies for anonymous visit counting.
  • AdSense cookies: Google AdSense places cookies to serve relevant ads. See Section 5.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. All tools continue to work without cookies.

5Google AdSense & Advertising

EraseAI displays advertisements served by Google AdSense to keep all tools free at $0. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your browsing activity.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at Google's Ad Settings or via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

6Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous traffic analytics. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Typography loading. Google may log font file requests.
  • HuggingFace CDN (jsdelivr.net): AI model files download on first use. Standard CDN access logs may be retained.
  • Cloudflare Pages: Our hosting provider handles request routing and may log IP addresses.

7Children's Privacy

EraseAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access to personal data, data deletion, and opt-out of personalised advertising. EU/EEA users have additional rights under GDPR. To exercise rights, contact us at the address below.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of EraseAI after changes constitutes acceptance.
