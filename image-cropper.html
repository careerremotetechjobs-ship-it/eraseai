Use 1:1 (square) for Instagram feed posts, 4:5 for portrait feed posts (takes up more screen space and gets more engagement), and 9:16 for Instagram Stories and Reels. All available as one-click presets. EraseAI is free — $0, no signup, no watermark.
YouTube recommends 16:9 aspect ratio for thumbnails (1280×720px minimum). Select the 16:9 preset in EraseAI, drag the crop box to frame your subject well, click Apply Crop, and download. Then compress the image to keep it under 2MB as required by YouTube.
Amazon requires main product images to be square (1:1 ratio) on a pure white background, at least 1000px on the longest side. Use the 1:1 crop preset to make your product image square, then use the Background Color Changer to add a white background — all free at $0.
LinkedIn profile photos display at 1:1 (square) ratio. Use the 1:1 preset to crop your headshot, ensuring your face is centered in the frame. LinkedIn also recommends a 3:1 ratio for banner/cover images.
