Frequently Asked Questions
Is EraseAI really free?
Yes, 100% free with no hidden limits, no signup, and no subscription required.
Are my images stored on your servers?
Never. All processing happens locally in your browser. We have zero access to your images.
Why is the first load slow?
The AI model (~170MB) downloads once on first use. After that it's cached and runs instantly.
What image formats are supported?
JPG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF up to 20MB in file size.
