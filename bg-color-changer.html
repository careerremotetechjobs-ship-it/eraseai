Yes, Amazon is strict about this. Main product images must have a pure white background — RGB 255,255,255, not light grey, not off-white, not cream. The product itself needs to fill at least 85% of the image frame. If your listing images get flagged or suppressed, a non-white background is one of the most common reasons.
White is the standard for most ecommerce platforms, but Shopify and Etsy give you more flexibility than Amazon does.
