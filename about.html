Our Mission

Professional image editing shouldn't cost a thing

Background removal used to require expensive software like Photoshop ($20+/month), paid subscriptions like remove.bg ($9+/month), or uploading your private photos to unknown servers. We built EraseAI to change that.

EraseAI is a completely free, browser-based suite of AI image tools. It was designed for photographers, ecommerce sellers, graphic designers, content creators, and anyone who needs professional image editing without paying for it — or sacrificing their privacy.

Because everything runs locally in your browser, your images stay on your device. We never see them, never store them, and never share them. That's not a promise — it's how the technology works. And it's all $0 forever.
