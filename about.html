Our Mission

Professional image editing shouldn't cost a thing

There's a frustrating gap between what most people need to do with images and what tools are available to them. Removing a background, resizing a photo for a specific platform, compressing a file for the web — these are straightforward tasks. But the software that handles them well either costs $20+ a month (Photoshop) or requires uploading your photos to a company's servers (remove.bg, Canva, Squoosh).

EraseAI started from a simple question: can these tools run entirely in the browser, using the computing power already sitting on your desk or in your pocket, for free? It turns out the answer is yes.

The AI models that power background removal have become small enough to run in a browser tab. The Canvas API in modern browsers is capable enough to handle compression, resizing, and format conversion without any server involvement. So that's how we built it.

Your images stay on your device from the moment you upload to the moment you download. We don't have an image processing server. There's no upload step where your photos leave your computer. That's not a marketing claim about privacy practices — it's just how the architecture works.

We keep the tools free by running Google advertising on the site. That's the entire business model. No paid tiers, no features locked behind a subscription, no usage credits to buy.

How It Works

Simple, powerful, completely private

1
Upload your image
Drag and drop or browse to upload any JPG, PNG, WEBP, or GIF file up to 20MB. The image loads directly into your browser — nothing is sent to a server at this stage, or any stage.
2
The tool runs on your device
For AI-powered tools like the background remover and photo enhancer, the AI model downloads once from HuggingFace CDN (about 170MB) and gets cached in your browser. After that first download, it runs locally on your hardware. Non-AI tools — resizing, compression, conversion, cropping — run instantly with no download needed.
3
Continue editing without re-uploading
After any tool finishes processing, a "Continue editing" section appears showing all the other tools. Click one and your image carries over automatically — no downloading and re-uploading between steps.
4
Download your result
Download as PNG, WEBP, or JPG — whichever format your project needs. Results download directly to your device with no watermarks and no quality loss from our end.
