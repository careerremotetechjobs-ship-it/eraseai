Our Mission

Professional background removal shouldn't cost a thing

Background removal used to require expensive software like Photoshop, paid subscriptions, or uploading your private photos to unknown servers. We built EraseAI to change that.

EraseAI is a completely free, browser-based AI tool that removes image backgrounds in seconds. It was designed for photographers, product sellers, graphic designers, content creators, and anyone who needs clean, transparent images without paying for it — or sacrificing their privacy.

Because everything runs locally in your browser, your images stay on your device. We never see them, never store them, and never share them. That's not just a promise — it's how the technology works.

How It Works

Simple, powerful, and transparent

1
Upload your image
Drag and drop or click to upload any JPG, PNG, WEBP, or GIF image up to 20MB in size.
2
AI model loads in your browser
The RMBG-1.4 ONNX model downloads from HuggingFace's CDN on your first use (~170MB) and is then cached by your browser — subsequent uses are instant.
3
Background is detected and removed
The AI analyses your image locally, identifies the foreground subject, and generates a precise transparency mask — all on your device.
4
Download your result
Preview the result on a checkerboard background confirming transparency, then download as PNG, WEBP, or JPG.
