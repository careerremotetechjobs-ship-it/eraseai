Amazon Image Requirements 2025

What Amazon requires for product images

Amazon has strict product image requirements. Failing to meet them can result in your listing being suppressed. Here's exactly what Amazon requires for main product images:

⬜
Background Color
Pure white — #ffffff
RGB 255,255,255. No off-white, grey, or gradients for main images.
📐
Minimum Size
1000px minimum
1500×1500px+ recommended to enable zoom feature. Square format preferred.
🖼️
Product Fill
85%+ of image
Product must fill at least 85% of the image frame. No excessive whitespace.
📄
File Format
JPEG or PNG
JPEG preferred. No watermarks, no text, no logos on main product image.
💾
File Size
Under 10MB
Keep under 10MB. Compress your image after editing to reduce size.
🎨
Color Mode
sRGB or CMYK
Use sRGB color mode for accurate color representation on all screens.

Violating these gets listings suppressed.
